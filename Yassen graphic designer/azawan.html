Radio Azawan — Brand Identity

Radio Azawan represents a comprehensive brand identity project for a contemporary radio station. This project involved creating a distinctive visual identity that captures the essence of modern broadcasting while maintaining cultural authenticity.

Design Approach

The design approach focused on creating a versatile brand system that works across various media platforms and applications. Key design objectives:

  • Memorable visual identity: The logo design needed to be instantly recognizable and work effectively across different scales, from social media avatars to billboard advertisements. Typography and color palette convey energy, modernity, and trustworthiness.
  • Flexible brand applications: The brand identity system includes station IDs, promotional materials, digital assets, and merchandise designs. Each application maintains brand consistency while adapting to specific use cases.
  • Strong market presence: The visual identity differentiates Radio Azawan in a competitive broadcasting landscape, creating a strong emotional connection with the target audience through compelling design elements.
