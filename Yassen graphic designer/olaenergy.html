OLA Energy Maroc — Summer Activation Campaign

Project Overview

This visual campaign was designed to promote OLA Energy’s summer activation, aimed at engaging customers through an energetic and vibrant atmosphere. The direction of the campaign reflects the essence of summer travel — open roads, sunshine, color, and the excitement of movement. The visual strategy enhances brand presence and encourages customer interaction through an upbeat, refreshing aesthetic.

The concept highlights:

  • Summer travel energy — vivid color palette and outdoor-inspired compositions.
  • Customer rewards & engagement — strong visual call-to-action.
  • Modern visual identity — dynamic design reflecting the renewed brand image of OLA Energy.
