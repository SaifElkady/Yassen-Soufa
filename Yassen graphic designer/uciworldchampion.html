Riding for the Flag – Morocco at the UCI Road World Championships 2025

Client

Royal Moroccan Cycling Federation (FRMC) & Sidi Ali Unlock

Project Overview

This key visual was developed to announce the official participation of the Moroccan national cycling team in the UCI Road World Championships 2025 held in Kigali, Rwanda. The creative direction highlights national pride, strength, speed, and global representation.

A heroic visual approach was used to position the athlete not just as a competitor, but as a symbol of Morocco's presence on the world stage. The composition merges modern sports aesthetics with identity elements — showcasing the team riding for the flag, for history, and for international recognition.
