Courses U.ma — Hyper U Maroc Delivery Campaign

Project Overview

This creative campaign was designed to promote Courses U.ma, Hyper U’s online delivery platform — highlighting fast, reliable grocery shopping delivered anywhere, anytime. The visual direction positions Courses U.ma as a futuristic, quick, and human-centered shopping solution.

The concept embraces a **beyond-the-limit delivery narrative**, visualising delivery that surpasses distance, obstacles, and expectations. This imaginative direction reinforces the promise: Order from home — we’ll deliver wherever you are.

Creative Strategy

  • Strong futuristic visual tone to express innovation & digital convenience.
  • Human-friendly delivery message — accessible, fast, and stress-free.
  • Brand storytelling focused on trust, speed & everyday practicality.
  • Clean layouts for ads, banners, social media, and delivery visuals.
